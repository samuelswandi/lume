## Why It Stayed With Me

I do not read this as a moral handbook. I read it as a vocabulary for status, strategy, and social maneuvering. Even when the advice is too cynical to adopt directly, it is useful to recognize the pattern.

## Notes I Kept Returning To

### Power is often theatrical

Perception, framing, and timing matter because social influence is partly performed before it is enforced.

### Reputation compounds

Once a reputation forms, it starts shaping how every later move gets interpreted.

### Strategy is contextual

The book is strongest when used diagnostically rather than literally. The laws are better as lenses than commandments.

## What I Took From It

- Notice the symbolic layer of social systems.
- Protect reputation as an operating asset.
- Read strategically without becoming mechanically cynical.
