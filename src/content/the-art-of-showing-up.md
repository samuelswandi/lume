## Why It Stayed With Me

This piece is simple in the best way. It keeps returning to an idea that is easy to underestimate: showing up is not passive. It is a real behavior with compounding effects.

## Notes I Kept Returning To

### Showing up matters

Presence sounds basic until you notice how much drift, delay, and half-effort come from not being fully there.

### Reliability creates trust before brilliance does

People often experience consistency long before they experience your best ideas.

### Attention is a form of respect

The essay works because it frames showing up as both personal discipline and relational signal.

## What I Took From It

- Showing up is a competitive advantage.
- Consistency is often more visible than talent.
- Presence changes the quality of work itself.
